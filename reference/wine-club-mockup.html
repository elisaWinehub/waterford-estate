FAQ

Membership questions

Can I skip or cancel a delivery?
Yes — you can pause, skip an upcoming shipment, or cancel your membership at any time from your account, with no lock-in contract.
How is shipping handled?
All Wine Club deliveries within South Africa ship free, with tracked courier delivery and temperature-conscious packaging. International membership is available on request — contact us for details.
Can I change my case size or wines?
Yes — you can adjust your quantity ahead of each delivery, and swap out individual bottles for alternatives from the current selection before your case ships.
What if a wine in my case is unavailable?
Our winemaking team selects a comparable substitute of equal or greater value from the current release, so your case is never short a bottle.
