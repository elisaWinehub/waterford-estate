Tasting Notes

Appearance

Deep, dense garnet with a youthful violet rim.

Nose

Blackcurrant and dark cherry, cedar, graphite and dark chocolate.

Palate

Dense yet polished, fine structured tannin, long savoury finish. Decant an hour before serving.
